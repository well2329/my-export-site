## Product Overview

This universal 7-function handlebar switch assembly is designed for reliable performance in all weather conditions. It includes left-side controls (headlight, turn signals, horn) and right-side controls (engine kill, starter). Ideal for OEM replacement or aftermarket upgrades.

![Full Assembly](/images/products/switch-1.jpg "7-Function Handlebar Switch Assembly")

## Key Features

- 7 functions: High/low beam, Left/Right turn signals, Horn, Engine kill switch, Starter  
- IP65 waterproof and dustproof rating  
- Ergonomic design with tactile feedback for comfortable use  
- 12V / 24V system compatible  
- LED backlight on buttons (optional)  
- Standard wiring harness for easy plug-and-play installation  
- Durable ABS + PC material with copper contacts
